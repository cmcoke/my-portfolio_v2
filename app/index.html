My Work Process

Research

Before I start coding a project I perform an extensive research so that I can have an understanding of a client's subscribers, the product/service that they are providing, their business goals and their competition in the respected market. Once completed a prototype of the email template is created in Adobe PhotoShop.

Coding

Using HTML and CSS, email templates are coded using an industry standard technique known as 'fluid hybrid design'. This technique allows the contents of an email to be responsive to the screen size that it is being viewed on. Regarding landing pages HTML and CSS are also used alongside JavaScript to add interactivity to the landing page if needed.

Testing

Before launching an email campaign I use a program called Litmus to perform a variety of tests on the email. These tests includes but are not limited to, ensuring that the email's contents are accessibile and ensure that the email is rendered consistently on various email clients.

Launch

When it comes to launching an email campaign I use MailChimp as the email service provider. With MailChimp I am able to perform tasks such as A/B testing, segmentation and get analytical reports that helps gauages an email success with it's subscribers.
