About Me

My short bio:

Born and raised in Kingston Jamaica I'm a self taught web developer who specializes in coding responsive email designs. When I first started learning web development I had originally wanted to get into the field of front-end development but after I had coded my first email and saw the instant gratification of praise from others I decided to give myself a try at becoming a professional email developer. When it comes to coding emails I strive to follow the best practices, standards and use programs such as Litmus to ensure that the email can be viewed at its best regardless of the email client that it is viewed on.

Admittedly, I don't have experience working in the field of email development however I would welcome the opportunity to contribute my skills and knowledge to a client's email project to the best of my abilites. Ideally I would like to become a valuable asset for a busy web agency but I'm open to working with any client in a temporary or permanent role. Last but not least, if it is a concern that I reside in Jamaica please note that I'm able to work in America as I have dual citizenship.
