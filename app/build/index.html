About Me

My short bio:

Born and raised in Kingston, Jamaica I'm a self taught web developer who specializes in coding web and email designs. When it comes to my work I strive to follow the best standards and practices that will ensure that my work not only looks good but is also functional for the end user regardless of what device they are using. This is especially the case when it comes to coding emails as not all email clients render emails the same. I use a coding technique known as fluid hybrid which allows an email's content to adjust it's appearance to fit the screen it is being viewed on. Before completing an email project I usually test it with a program called Litmus which allows me to ensure that the email can be viewed at it's best regardless of the email client that is viewed on.

Admittedly, I don't have working experience in the fields of web or email development however I would welcome the opportunity to contribute my skills and knowledge to a client's project to the best of my abilites. Ideally I would like to become a valuable asset for a busy web agency but I'm open to working with any client in a temporary or permanent role. Last but not least, if it is a concern that I reside in Jamaica please note that I'm able to work in America as I have dual citizenship.
